# FeatherPunch

A role-based life tracking app that helps you punch into what you are doing, then review where your time went with clear comparisons, patterns, and context.

> "You do not live one life. You live many roles. FeatherPunch helps you see how those roles are really taking shape."

## What it does

- **Create roles** — Sleeping, Working, Studying, Building, Rest, Family, etc.
- **Punch in / punch out** — one active role at a time, with smart switching
- **Review Insights** — rolling 7/30/90 day views, previous-period comparisons, key takeaway, role breakdowns, and patterns
- **Understand changes** — see what increased or dropped and which role likely drove it
- **Estimate earnings** — optional hourly-rate based earning estimates for paid roles
- **Inspect and edit logs** — filter by range/role, edit sessions, and export CSV

## Product philosophy

- **Track first, reflect second** — quick session control on Home, then meaningful analysis in Insights.
- **Truthful over flashy** — avoid fake claims when data is sparse; comparisons use clear thresholds.
- **Local-first** — no account required, data stays on-device.
- **Calm and practical** — helpful context without noisy pressure.
