# FeatherPunch

A life-allocation app that lets you punch into the roles you live, then shows where your time is truly going — especially how much belongs to **you** versus **everyone else**.

> "You do not live one life. You live many roles. This app shows which ones are truly yours."

## What it does

- **Create roles** — Sleeping, Working, Studying, Building, Rest, Family, etc.
- **Tag each role** — "Me" (for yourself) or "Other" (for others)
- **Punch in / punch out** — one active role at a time, with smart switching
- **See what life looks like** — honest charts, reflective insights, and a Me vs Other split
